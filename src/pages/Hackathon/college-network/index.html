FAQs

This FAQ section provides answers to common questions about the Grand Hackathon organized by GP Kashipur in collaboration with IT Off-Topics.

  • The Grand Hackathon will take place over two days, from November 26 to November 27, 2024. Participants will have exactly 48 hours to work on their projects.

  • Yes, teamwork is mandatory! Each team should consist of ideally five members from the same college. Mixed-year groups are allowed, but all members must belong to the same institution.

  • All projects must be built from scratch during the hackathon. If any prior work or code is used, it must be clearly stated. Plagiarism will result in disqualification.

  • Winners will receive a beautiful trophy along with certificates for each group member, enhancing their resumes and showcasing their achievement!
